Would you consider sharing your experiences with these products?

What A Consumer Opinion Panel Does

Our Bladder Leakage & Incontinence Consumer Opinion Panel is a group of consumers like you, who experience bladder leakage and use absorbent products to manage it.

These consumers provide their feedback on the product by:

  1. using it in their daily life
  2. answering questions about their experience with the product

Through sharing their insights, our Panel is helping to shape the future of the these important products.

We invite you to join our Panel. Your opinions matter, and we want to hear your insights.

How It Works

  1. When a study becomes available, we send you an email invite.
  2. You answer some pre-qualifying questions to confirm that study is appropriate for you. If it is, you read and sign a consent form and a confidentiality agreement.
  3. We ship the product to your home.
  4. We have a phone (or video) call with you to review the study and answer any questions you may have.
  5. You use the product and answer the product evaluation questions.
  6. Upon completion of the product evaluation, you will receive compensation as a thank you for your time and insights.

How to Participate

  1. Sign up to join the Bladder Leakage & Incontinence Consumer Opinion Panel using the Contact Form to the right, or call the 800 number.
  2. You will receive a welcome email with a link to a brief product usage survey. Complete the survey. This helps us understand what types of products you use, so that we can send appropriate study invitations to you.
  3. As product evaluations come up that fit your product usage, you will receive study invitation emails. Invitations include a description of the study, the incentive amount, and a link to a brief questionnaire which help us determine if you meet that study's specifications.
  4. Upon completion of each study participation, you receive compensation via an emailed virtual incentive card.
